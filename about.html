About

Miles is an Ithaca, New York native living in San Jose, California.

He has held positions in sales, social work, auto mechanics, cooking, social media, and psychology, and uses this wide range of knowledge to bring meaningful insights to web and mobile platforms.

His goal is to help people navigate the evolving digital landscape by designing meaningful experiences for users.

He enjoys building and flying drones, track days, martial arts, tinkering, and technology.
